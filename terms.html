⚠️ Important Safety Warning

Freediving and breath-hold training carry inherent risks, including shallow water blackout, hypoxia, and drowning — which can result in serious injury or death.

NEVER train alone. Always freedive and breath-hold train with a qualified buddy who is capable of performing a rescue.

NEVER hyperventilate before a breath-hold. Hyperventilation suppresses the urge to breathe without increasing oxygen, dramatically increasing the risk of losing consciousness underwater.

Fathom Blue provides training guidance and educational content only. It is not a substitute for in-person instruction from a qualified freediving instructor. We strongly recommend completing a certified freediving course (AIDA, PADI, or equivalent) before engaging in breath-hold training.

Always train within your limits. Never push beyond what is safe. If in doubt, don't dive.
